About Me

I came to the United States when I was five, after living in Peru for a few years. It took me about a year to fully learn English. I have my parents to thank for that. From the age of 5 until the age of 18 I played soccer at a competitive level. I used to travel to states throughout the Eastern coast, and the last championship I played was in Florida, where my team came second. It wasn't until the Summer of 2020 that I found my passion for Computer Science. Before this, I had never coded anything, but I was always on my computer when I wasn't playing soccer or video games. During this Summer, someone I looked up to got me into this fascinating field, which I then researched heavily, later making Python my main language. Since then, I transferred to John Jay in the Fall of 2020 and completely immersed myself into this field.

But, to cut things short, now I enjoy making iOS Applications. I am currently working on a Crypto app using the SwiftUI Framework. Whenever I'm not studying for class or buildings apps, I do Leetcode problems to get better at technical interviews.
